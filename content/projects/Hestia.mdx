Website created to depict the sheer amount of crime in Toronto using statistics from 2014-2019. The data is plotted on a map to pinpoint exact location of the crimes and also on graphs to show the severity of crimes in some neighbourhoods.

###### Source of Data

The data is fetched directly from the Toronto Police DB.
